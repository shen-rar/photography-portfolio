About Me

The Eye
Behind
the Lens

I'm Praveen Ganesh, a visual storyteller based in Chennai, India. My work spans portrait, wildlife, and street photography — each frame a search for authentic light and honest emotion.

Drawn to the quiet dignity of everyday people and the raw energy of the natural world, I craft images that linger long after the shutter clicks. Available for commissions, collaborations, and editorial projects that believe in the power of visual truth.
